FAQs

What is Crop Commerce?

It is e-commerce website with the aim to create awareness among people about the various efficient techniques for farming and also the sale of products.

What features does Crop Commerce offer?

It provides a platform to learn about different farming techniques and steps involved for sustainable agriculture. It also acts a forum which allows user to connect with us and also buy products.

Is Crop Commerce free to use?

Yes, Crop Commerce offers free access to most of our resources and tools.

What type of farming does Crop Commerce supports?

We at Crop Commerce support wide range of farming which includes organic farming, animal husbandry, apiculture, horticulture and agro-forestry.

Can I get one-one consultation on Crop Commerce?

Yes, Crop Commerce offers personalized advice from agricultural experts. You can book one-on-one consultation sessions to discuss specific issues and get tailored solutions.
